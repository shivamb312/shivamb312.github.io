Why Vote For Me ?

Student Life, the major system, and transparency with the student body are three aspects that this school severely lacks. Student Life is a major aspect of every university in the country. UW is known as a commuter school, and I want to change that culture. Not only that, but the major system is also broken. If elected, I will invest resources into student engagement, alleviate the stress of the major system, and increase transparency to students.

I have been a part of many student clubs on campus, so I know what it's like to be in a group of people who want to share their passions. I am currently serving as the Esports Coordinator for the Gaming Association at UW, I am the Internal Vice President and Operations Director for Zeta Beta Tau, I played on our club Ultimate Frisbee team, and I founded the Rocket League club before I even began attending UW. Throughout my time in all these organizations, I saw how much club culture can empower and inspire. I want to bring that to the rest of the school.

I have spoken to many students and I have listened to their concerns. I know what students want and what they do not. Here is what I plan to accomplish:

Work with the Director of Legislative affairs to increase the Washington State Financial aid budget
Ensure that the online learning tools that have been introduced in spring quarter are to be utilized once in-person classes resume and improve the accessibility of our classes
Grow the marginalized communities on campus with the Director of Diversity Affairs by ensuring that every student is represented
Work with the Director of University Affairs and the Provost Advisory Committee for Students to create a more holistic major application process
Create an ASUW Discord and empower /r/udub subreddit with ASUW resources and work with them to serve as new channel of communications and to strengthen the online community for UW students
Bring UW into broadcasting space on YouTube, Twitch, and Facebook to create fun and entertaining content that also informs students on what is happening at the school
Work with the Director of Programming and Director of Community Relations to restructure the RSO system to give individual clubs and students more of a voice
Create more engagement opportunities with ASUW officers for the public, such as weekly community game nights and seasonal panels
Move the UWPD off-campus boundary from 45th to 47th or even 50th
Repave the streets in the Greek System
